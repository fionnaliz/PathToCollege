What is the SAT?

Definition/ Better Understanding of it

A test of a student's academic skills, used for admission to US colleges. S- Scholastic, A- Aptitude, T- Test

What is the ACT?

Definition/ Better Understanding of it

A standardized test for high school achievement and college admissions in the United States. A- American, C- College, T- Testing

What is a GPA?

Definition/ Better Understanding of it

A number representing the average value of the accumulated final grades earned in courses over time. G- Grade P- Point A- Average.
